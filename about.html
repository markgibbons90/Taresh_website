About Taresh

Taresh Limited is a privately owned company located in N. Ireland specialising in the supply of a wide range of raw materials. The company was founded in 1994, with the sole aim of providing excellent customer service, combined with transparency and simplicity of offer. We specialise in the sourcing and supply of an extensive portfolio of speciality and general chemicals used in a diverse range of industry applications. Our products find application in:

  • Animal Nutrition
  • Pharmaceutical
  • Personal care
  • Food & Beverages
  • Flavour & Fragrance
  • Cosmetics
  • Sports and Lifestyle Nutrition

With a global warehouse network, we can guarantee minimum lead times and reliable delivery. We maintain stock at strategic locations within the UK and Europe to serve our varied customer base and ensure that material is available when our customer’s need it, even if at very short notice. Our business has been built on a customer focused philosophy and our customers range from small businesses to multi-national companies. We act as an agent and distributor and the manufacturers we work with are located in the USA, China, Europe and India.

Our combined expertise of over 20 years of sourcing materials is our strength, and we are constantly expanding our product range putting our experience into practice to locate new sources of chemicals. With close, longstanding supplier relationships, we are able to offer our customers a fast and efficient service. We pride ourselves on our flexibility and ability to consistently offer quality products at competitive prices and we appreciate the importance of delivering on time and on budget.

We have partnerships with key raw material manufacturers, many of which have been working with Taresh Ltd for well over a decade. We also audit these premises personally along with other well recognised independent auditors. Each product provided comes with a full C of A, and comprehensive specification sheets are also available. Detailed information can be provided relating to the relevant pharmacopoeia’s, allergens, GMO status, Vegetarian and Vegan status, and kosher certifications where applicable. We also have available the MSDS and COSHH data when required. From both UK and European mainland warehouse locations, Taresh can promptly and effectively meet all of your requirements.

From our wide-range of stocked products in our UK warehouses, we can deliver to most areas of the UK promptly and long-established relationships with a number of logistics providers allows us to deliver your product where and when you need it. So, whatever your requirement, we’re willing to listen and are here to try and help.
